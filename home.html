Privacy Policy

Awardify uses the Spotify Web API to receive data from your Spotify profile. By choosing to use Awardify, you agree to the use of your Spotify account username and data for your top artists and tracks.

None of the data used by Awardify is stored or collected anywhere, and it is not shared with any third parties. All information is used only for displaying your award show.

If you would like to remove Awardify's permissions to access your Spotify data, please do so here.
